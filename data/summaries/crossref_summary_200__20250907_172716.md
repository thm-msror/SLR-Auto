## 4. Overall Trends

There seems to be a growing interest in leveraging large language models (LLMs) and multimodal approaches for various tasks within video retrieval systems. There is also a focus on enhancing existing methods through iterative navigation, cross interaction networks, and attention mechanisms. However, there appears to be a decline in traditional content-based image retrieval techniques and interactive video retrieval systems. Additionally, there is an increasing emphasis on spatial-temporal object understanding and event localization, particularly in relation to audiovisual events. Finally, there is a rising trend towards utilizing transfer learning and incorporating graph neural networks into these systems.
