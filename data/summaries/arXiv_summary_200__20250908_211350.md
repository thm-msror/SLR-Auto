#### Overall Trend Summary:

The field of video retrieval augmented by large language models continues to grow rapidly with increased focus on improving accuracy and efficiency especially when dealing with longer videos. There is also growing interest in incorporating various forms of auxiliary information into the retrieval process. While there are still gaps regarding certain types of content or applications (e.g., real-time streaming scenarios), researchers seem committed to addressing these challenges through innovative solutions involving advanced machine learning architectures and novel training strategies.
