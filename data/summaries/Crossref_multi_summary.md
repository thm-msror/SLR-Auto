### Overall Trends

The field of video search by natural language query has evolved significantly over time, focusing more on integrating semantic understanding into various stages of the process. While there have been advancements in action recognition and semantic gap bridging, there still seems to be room for improvement in handling diverse domains like sign languages and marine videos effectively.
